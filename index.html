About

Anemia: low red blood cells or hemoglobin, causing oxygen shortage.
Symptoms: fatigue, weakness, shortness of breath, pale skin.
Causes: nutritional deficiencies, diseases, blood loss. Treatments vary.

Hemoglobin

Men: 13.2–16.6 g/dL, Women: 11.6–15 g/dL

MCH

Normal range: 27.5–33.2 picograms

MCHC

Normal range: 33.4–35.5 g/dL

MCV

Normal range: 80–100 fL
